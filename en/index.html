Questions Frequently

Most of the inquiries we receive are from our customers

What internet speed is required for smooth operation?

To ensure an excellent viewing experience without any buffering, we recommend that your internet speed be at least 15 Mbps, especially for watching 4K channels.

Can I run the subscription on more than one device?

You can set up the subscription on multiple devices (such as a monitor and a mobile phone), but the subscription cannot be run on two devices at the same time unless you request a dedicated package for that purpose.

How do I receive my subscription details after payment?

Once payment is completed or you contact us via WhatsApp, the technical support team will send you the login details (username and password) along with a simple explanation of how to operate it within minutes.

What devices and applications do you support?

We support all smart screens (Samsung, LG, Android TV), Apple TV devices, smartphones (iPhone and Android), and computers via applications such as IPTV Smarters, IBO Player, and others.
